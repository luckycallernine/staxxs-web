The problem

Teaching 30 students at once is hard

You can only be in one place at a time. Students go quiet. Work happens — or it doesn't — and you can't always tell which until it's too late.

  • 👀
    You can't see who's actually working Walking the room gives you a snapshot. By the time you've done a lap, everything has changed.
  • 🙋
    Students won't always put their hand up Some are stuck but silent. Others are done and waiting. You don't know who's who.
  • 📋
    Pacing a class is guesswork Moving everyone on when half the room isn't ready — or holding back students who finished five minutes ago.
What Staxxs does

Your whole class.
One screen.

Everything you need to run a focused, visible, productive class — without the chaos.

📊
Live teacher dashboard

See every student's card simultaneously. Status indicators show who's typing, who's done, who's stuck, and who's idle — all at a glance.

🃏
Card-based tasks

Build stacks of question cards for students to work through. Add prompts, set the order, and import in bulk via CSV for fast setup.

🔀
Two flow modes

Student flow lets learners move at their own pace. Teacher flow locks the class to your card — perfect for guided instruction and class discussions.

🙋
Question flags

Students flag when they need help or mark themselves done. No hands required. You see it instantly on the dashboard.

📤
Export to Google Docs

Export any student's responses straight to a Google Doc for assessment, feedback, or portfolios. One click, formatted and ready.

⚡
Instant student join

Students join with a name and a code — no accounts, no emails, no app installs. Works on phones, tablets, and laptops.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Sign up as a teacher and create a room for your class. Takes thirty seconds.

2

3
